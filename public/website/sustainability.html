Sustainability

Operating in harmony with nature and community.

Environmental Stewardship

The Standard Gauge Railway traverses some of Kenya's most iconic landscapes, including the Tsavo National Park. Afristar is deeply committed to ensuring that our operations have minimal impact on the ecosystem.

  • Wildlife Corridors: We maintain fences and underpasses designed to allow wildlife to migrate freely beneath the railway tracks without danger.
  • Noise Reduction: We implement noise barriers in sensitive areas to disturb wildlife and communities as little as possible.
  • Waste Management: Strict protocols are in place for the disposal of waste from our trains and stations, ensuring zero pollution to the environment.
