Our History & Mandate

World-Class Railway Management

Africa Star Railway Operation Company Limited (Afristar) was incorporated in 2017 with a singular mandate: to operate and maintain the Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) of Kenya. Spanning 592KM from the port city of Mombasa to Nairobi and further inland to Naivasha/Suswa, the SGR is the backbone of Kenya's modern transport infrastructure.

Since inception, we have successfully transported millions of passengers and tons of cargo, significantly reducing logistical costs and travel times across the region.

Localization & Capacity Building

A key pillar of Afristar's strategy is the transfer of skills and technology to Kenyan nationals. We have achieved over 90% localization of our workforce, with continuous training programs ensuring that the future of Kenya's railways is in Kenyan hands.
